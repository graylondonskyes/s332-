The Problem

Most contractor disputes start with missing records, conflicting hour claims, or no documentation. By the time there’s a problem, it’s already expensive.

The Solution

ContractorOps provides companies a secure platform to:

Self-Reported Hours

Contractors submit voluntary activity records with descriptions and documentation.

Admin Approval

Admins review submissions, approve or reject entries, and maintain a clean audit trail.

Incentive Tracking

Set stipend thresholds and eligibility rules; system flags qualifying contractors automatically.

AI Activity Summaries

Generate neutral, administrative summaries of activity for easy dispute review.

Exportable Reports

Download CSV or PDF reports for audits, accounting, or internal tracking.

Secure Storage

All attachments and records are stored securely and accessible only to authorized users.
