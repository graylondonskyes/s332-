A process built for speed, control, and credibility.

The goal is simple: ship a site that closes, then evolve it monthly with disciplined upgrades and content that compounds trust. Every step is designed to prevent “endless revisions” and protect brand posture.

1

Authority + Offer Audit

Identify the money pages (services, booking, pricing, locations) and define one primary offer stack with supporting upsells. Output: page map + CTA map + proof plan.

2

Demo-First Build

A working demo is produced first: full visual direction, real copy blocks, and conversion flow. Output: shareable demo link + edit list + approval gates.

3

Launch + Indexing Readiness

Launch with clean structure: metadata, internal linking, section hierarchy, mobile readiness, speed discipline, and CTA instrumentation-ready layout.

4

Monthly Control Loop

Monthly production: edits, new sections, new pages, content drops, and reporting. Output: delivered work log + next upgrades list.

Credibility Standards

Built to look legitimate — and stay legitimate.

  • Proof modules: reviews, before/after galleries, staff highlights, FAQs, policies
  • Buyer clarity: pricing logic, what’s included, timelines, expectations, boundaries
  • Operational readiness: booking flow, service areas, contact routing, update cadence
  • Brand continuity: consistent typography, palette, spacing, and visual hierarchy
  • Risk control: no fragile plugins required; lean scripts; rollback discipline
