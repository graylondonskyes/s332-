New to SEO? Here’s the Practical Version

Straight answers on what SEO is, what matters most, and what a real strategy looks like.

Local SEO helps you show up for location-intent searches (maps + local results). It’s about consistent business data, location pages, reviews, proximity signals, and strong service relevance.

What we optimizeLocation pages, business listings, review velocity, local schema, map relevance.
Best forService businesses, multi-location brands, local storefronts, regional providers.

Ecommerce SEO focuses on category architecture, product discoverability, collection pages, internal linking, and content that supports buying decisions — while preserving speed and crawl budget.

What we optimizeCollections, product templates, faceted navigation, schema, content hubs.
Best forStores with many SKUs, seasonal demand, paid traffic dependency, scaling catalogs.

Enterprise SEO is about scale: governance, templates, technical automation, and cross-team execution. The strategy must work across thousands of URLs without breaking performance.

What we optimizeTemplate rules, crawl budgets, internal linking systems, governance, migrations.
Best forLarge sites, franchises, publishers, SaaS libraries, complex architectures.
