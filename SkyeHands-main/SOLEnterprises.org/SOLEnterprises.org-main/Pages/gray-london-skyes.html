The Builder

Gray London Skyes is the founder and CEO of SOLEnterprises, a multi-industry holding company built to unify strategy, operations, and delivery across high-accountability environments. His operating footprint spans Arizona, Texas, Colorado, and Illinois—focused on real outcomes: contracts secured, execution stabilized, and growth made repeatable.

Contracts & Partnerships

Across multiple lanes of regulated logistics and service delivery, Skyes has secured and executed private contracts and partnerships with recognized organizations including Roadie (a UPS partner), HopSkipDrive (DHS-certified), CVS Pharmacy, Walmart, Best Buy, OnePoint Patient Care, Globe Life – Liberty National Division, and InVida Elite LLC—alongside strategic work supporting COSA (Couriers of San Antonio).
