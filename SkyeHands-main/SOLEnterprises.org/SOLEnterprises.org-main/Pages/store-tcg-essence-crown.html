Core Rules

Objective: Reduce opponent’s Essence to 0 or achieve special win conditions.

Essence: Your life total; start at 23, can be healed, damaged, or paid as cost.

Kundalini Level (KL): Energy resource, max 31. Recalculated each turn.

God Charges: Gained at 13 KL threshold, max 3, usable after turn 3.

Turn Structure

  • Start Phase: Ready permanents, draw, recalc KL, God Threshold check.
  • Main Phase 1: Play cards, activate abilities.
  • Combat Phase: Attack/block, assign damage.
  • Main Phase 2: Additional plays.
  • End Phase: Resolve triggers, lose unused KL.

Zones Explained

  • Deity Panel (Essence/KL/God Charges)
  • Domain Zone (active Domain)
  • Relic/Support Zone (buffs/utilities)
  • Shard Row & Avatar Line (combat units & ramp)
  • Veiled Deck (draw pile)
  • Crypt (discard/used cards)
  • Null Zone (exiled cards)

Card Types

  • Deity: Leader, defines starting stats & abilities
  • Domains: Permanent environment effects
  • Shards: Ramp / energy bonuses
  • Avatars: Combat units (Power/Guard)
  • Spells/Rites: One-shot or ritual effects
  • Relics/Support: Buffs, static effects
  • Tokens / Echo: Temporary units
  • Essence/Resource cards: Manipulate life and energy
