FAQ

Clear answers, clean expectations. This is designed to protect you from confusion and protect us from mismatched scope.

Do you do taxes? ›
Northstar focuses on back office control (workflows, invoicing, bookkeeping systems, KPI frameworks, cleanup). If tax filing support is needed, we can coordinate with licensed partners where appropriate. Nothing on this page is tax advice.
What does “KPI-ready” mean? ›
It means your books are structured so reports are actually meaningful: consistent categories, clear invoice mapping, disciplined vendor coding, and dashboards that are built around operator questions (run-rate, AR aging, margin signals, cost discipline, trend direction).
Can you fix messy historical books? ›
We can run a cleanup plan, but scope depends on how many months/years are affected, volume of transactions, and document availability. The fastest win is usually: stabilize the system going forward, then clean history in batches.
What if I already have a bookkeeper? ›
Perfect. We can implement the system and SOPs, then your bookkeeper runs the cadence. Northstar’s value is structure + controls — so execution stays clean even when staff changes.
What tools do you use? ›
We design systems that are tool-agnostic. Your stack may include accounting software, invoicing tools, spreadsheets, or lightweight dashboards. The goal is always: clear workflows, consistent categories, and repeatable reporting.
Do you guarantee results? ›
We don’t promise outcomes we can’t control. What we do guarantee is: clear deliverables, clear SOPs, and a system built to support disciplined execution. Results depend on implementation and ongoing adherence.
