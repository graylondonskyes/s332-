Daily Rituals

Pick a short ritual to center before a deep session. Rituals can be timed and tracked. Progress persists in your browser.

30s Breath Anchor

Three rounds: inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 6s. Quick reset to lower arousal and increase focus.

5‑minute Focus Kick

Light movement, posture check, 60s single-task planning, then lock for first pomodoro.

Micro Recovery

Two-minute sensory reset: eyes off screen, cool water sip, slow breathing.
