FAQ

Is income guaranteed?

No. Contractor pool eligibility provides access to paid roles and pathways. Compensation is performance-based and not guaranteed.

Will the LLC be approved before graduation?

Filing submission is handled during the program once intake is complete. State approval timing varies. Filings are trackable.

Is this a school program?

No. Solara is a private academy delivered through approved host facilities and/or Solara office sessions.

What if a student falls behind?

Weekly 1:1 coaching exists specifically to correct trajectory. Completion requires meeting deliverable and certification requirements.
