Walkthrough

          
How to use this console

        

        
      

      

        

          1) Import a lead sheet (CSV/XLSX) or add a lead manually.

          2) Select the lead — the center panel loads it automatically.

          3) Press “Start Call” — the Call Runner begins Step 1 and walks the call line-by-line.

          4) Speak the script and click the option that matches what happens. Routing is automatic.

          5) Log outcomes at the end and press “Complete + Log Call” — status updates automatically.

          6) Build the Authority Assessment (right panel), copy it, paste into email/CRM, optionally open email draft.

          7) Export to send managers lead + activity logs.
        

        

        

          Call win condition: capture email and secure commitment to submit the Authority Assessment today or tomorrow morning.
          This is not a full close on the first call.
