What SkyeCares Does

SkyeCares is a benefits concierge workflow built to reduce confusion and prevent expensive “missed details.” You get structure, tracking, and an organized Coverage Vault you can actually use.

Coverage Vault Setup

We build a structured vault index and trackers so your coverage is understandable at a glance.

  • Policy + card library map (what, where, who to call)
  • Key contacts (carrier, HR/benefits, provider, pharmacy)
  • Premiums, deductibles, OOP max, copays snapshot
  • Dependents & provider list reference

Open Enrollment War Room

A guided decision flow for enrollment season: compare plans, spot traps, and lock your choice with clarity.

  • Plan comparison grid (cost vs risk)
  • Questions-to-ask checklist (carrier/HR)
  • Change-impact review (new meds, new providers, travel)
  • Enrollment deadline and action calendar

Claims & EOB Tracker Support

Track claims, EOBs, and denials with a clean timeline and “next action” routing.

  • Claim status timeline + reference numbers
  • Billed vs allowed vs paid vs patient responsibility
  • Denial flags + appeal deadline tracker
  • Document checklist (EOBs, receipts, letters)
Support note: SkyeCares provides organization and decision support. If you want us to coordinate calls, compile questions, or assemble appeal packets, that scope is defined in your SOW/Deliverables for your project.
