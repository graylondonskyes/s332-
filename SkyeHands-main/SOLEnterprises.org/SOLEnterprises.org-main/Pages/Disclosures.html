Disclosures

No Professional Advice

Information provided on our pages, in messages, in templates, or inside tools is for general informational purposes and operational guidance only.

No legal advice

Skyes Over London LC is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. If you need legal advice, consult a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction.

No tax/financial advice

We do not provide tax, accounting, investment, or financial advice. Consult a qualified CPA, tax professional, or financial advisor as appropriate.

Any decisions you make based on information from our Site are your responsibility. If professional advice is required, you should seek it before taking action.

Affiliate & Referral Disclosure

Skyes Over London LC may reference, recommend, or link to third-party products or services (for example: software tools, hosting providers, payment processors, vendors, or operational platforms).

Affiliate relationships.

In some cases, we may receive compensation if you click a link, purchase a product, or sign up for a service through an affiliate or referral link. This does not necessarily increase your cost, and we do not accept compensation in exchange for false claims.

When a relationship is material to a recommendation, we aim to disclose it clearly on the same page or within the same flow where the recommendation appears.

Referral fees & procurement.

Some engagements may involve vendor sourcing or procurement support. If vendor referral fees, placement fees, or commissions apply to a specific engagement, those terms are disclosed in the applicable agreement, SOW, or project attachment.

Third-Party Services & External Links

Our Site may link to or integrate with third-party services. We do not control third-party websites, pricing, uptime, security practices, or policies.

  • Third-party services may change features, availability, or terms without notice.
  • Third-party content is provided “as is” and is governed by the third party’s policies.
  • Your use of third-party services is at your own risk.
Pass-through costs.

If you purchase third-party services (hosting, domains, subscriptions, ads, filings, vendor costs), you are responsible for those charges, unless a written agreement explicitly states Skyes Over London LC will pay them on your behalf.

Results, Performance & Testimonials

Business outcomes vary. Any examples, projections, or statements about potential results (including operational efficiency, revenue impact, conversion improvements, rankings, or lead volume) are illustrative and not guarantees.

No guarantee of results.

We cannot guarantee specific performance outcomes because results depend on external factors such as market conditions, competition, platform policies, budgets, timelines, and client-provided inputs.

Testimonials.

Testimonials and client statements reflect individual experiences and may not represent typical results. If testimonials are used, they are not promises of future performance.

Where performance standards or service-level commitments exist, they are defined only in written agreements (and not in general marketing materials).

Availability, Scheduling & Capacity

Scheduling and delivery timelines may depend on resource availability, project complexity, and timely access to required inputs. If your engagement includes deadlines, those are controlled by your SOW/Deliverables and may require timely approvals and information.

Client inputs matter.

Delays caused by missing access, approvals, content, or stakeholder feedback can affect delivery timelines and outcomes. If a project is paused due to missing inputs, schedules may shift.

Tools, Automation & Output Limitations

Some services may include tools, templates, automation, or AI-assisted systems. Outputs may require review, adaptation, and validation before use in real-world scenarios.

  • Automated outputs may contain errors, omissions, or require jurisdiction-specific adjustments.
  • Operational templates should be reviewed for fit, compliance, and accuracy before deployment.
  • Platform policies (hosting providers, social networks, marketplaces) may change and affect performance.
Security note.

Do not submit sensitive credentials, payment data, or confidential information through unsecured channels unless expressly instructed. Use approved secure methods where provided.
