Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our elite sanitation services.

What makes Obsidian Cartage different from standard waste services?

+
We specialize in discrete, high-standards environments where appearance, timing, and confidentiality are critical. Our unmarked vehicles, guaranteed service windows, and white-glove approach set us apart from commodity waste management providers.

How does your scheduling work?

+
We work with each client to establish optimal service windows—typically late-night or early-morning hours. Once established, we maintain strict adherence to these schedules with backup protocols to ensure zero disruption to your operations.

What types of facilities do you serve?

+
Our clientele includes boutique medical clinics, research laboratories, professional office towers, luxury residential developments, and any organization that requires premium waste management with absolute discretion.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

+
All team members undergo background checks and confidentiality training. We use unmarked vehicles, maintain secure chain of custody documentation, and can accommodate additional security protocols as required by your facility.

What is your emergency response capability?

+
We maintain 24/7 emergency response capability with dedicated priority channels for urgent situations. Our average response time for emergency calls is under 90 minutes in metropolitan service areas.

How do I get started?

+
Email us to request a confidential consultation. We’ll confirm your service window, handling requirements, documentation preferences, and any special protocols—then issue a tailored service proposal.
