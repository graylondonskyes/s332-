5 QUESTIONS
Qualification first. If they don’t qualify, you don’t pitch.

Use the 5 questions exactly. Then use the scorecard. The scorecard tells you the next move: submit now, nurture, or disqualify.

The 5 Questions (Do Not Skip)
1) What do you sell and what do you charge right now?
2) Biggest issue: inconsistent leads, credibility, nonpayment, or scope creep?
3) Do you already have an LLC + enforceable contracts — or are you operating freestyle?
4) If the system is installed, will you execute for 36 months with discipline?
5) Can you commit to a fixed schedule if deliverables are proof-based and enforced?

If they can’t answer #5 cleanly, you stop. You don’t beg. You disqualify or nurture.
