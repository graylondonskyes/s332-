What does “spirit + receipts” mean?

We hold room for spirit, myth, and memory — and we also track threads: source notes, lineage connections, historical receipts, and explicit assumptions. The portal format makes the structure visible, so the reader can evaluate, build, and extend the work.

Is this academic-only?

No. It’s rigorous, but built for use: artists, builders, organizers, educators, and world-makers. The writing is designed to move — and the portal supplies the receipts, reading routes, and frameworks.

How do “research engines” work?

Each title has a portal: glossary, index, claim threads, “start here” routes, and build tools. It’s a navigable system — so new readers enter cleanly, advanced readers go deeper, and builders apply immediately.

Can I license a portal for my org/studio?

Yes. We can build org-specific portals (curriculum lanes, study sequences, internal indices, and operational frameworks). Scope and terms are handled in an SOW/Deliverables attachment.
