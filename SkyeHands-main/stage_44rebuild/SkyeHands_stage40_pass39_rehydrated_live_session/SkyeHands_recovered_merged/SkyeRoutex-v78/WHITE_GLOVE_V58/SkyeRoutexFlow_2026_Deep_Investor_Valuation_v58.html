Valuation conclusion

$6,850,000

Investor-style software and infrastructure valuation for the Skye RoutexFlow platform, excluding vehicles, live fleet assets, signed revenue contracts, and cash-on-hand.

This asset is not a normal route planner. The codebase has become a vertical operating system for premium private-driver and white-glove service. The directive requires one canonical chain from request to dispatch to route execution to closeout to receipt/export/sync, and the current package contains the module stack and backend contract scaffolding to support that architecture.

What the platform does

  • Accepts premium white-glove ride requests and converts them into canonical booking records.
  • Maintains canonical service profiles for individuals, households, businesses, VIP, medical, and executive riders.
  • Maintains canonical driver, vehicle, pricing-snapshot, booking, membership, payout, sync, and proof records.
  • Supports retail, reserve, on-demand, hourly standby, airport, errand, recurring, and membership-backed service patterns.
  • Tracks favorite-driver requests truthfully through matched, unavailable, override, acceptance, and decline states.
  • Generates quote previews and freezes pricing snapshots onto confirmed bookings.
  • Runs dispatch, assignment, reassignment, route materialization, conflict checking, and live execution from one system.
  • Captures white-glove service events like assistance, wait/standby, handoff, service recovery, cancellation, and no-show proof.
  • Writes receipts, service summaries, client transparency packets, and member statements from the canonical chain.
  • Provides restore, merge, duplicate review, collision audit, route-chain audit, and acceptance-proof machinery.
  • Exposes backend contract lanes for bookings, memberships, dispatch, payments, and sync rather than fake UI-only hooks.
  • Includes Academy/tutorial coverage so the product can teach itself in-app.

Why this commands a platform premium

  • It replaces multiple operational systems at once: CRM continuity, pricing engine, booking intake, dispatch, execution, payout, proof, member drawdown, dispute reconstruction, and restore/merge tooling.
  • It is built as a truth-preserving chain. Bookings, profiles, drivers, vehicles, memberships, docs, and sync states are tied together rather than fragmented across separate tools.
  • It has offline-first operating value. Field execution can continue even when website or sync lanes are degraded.
  • It has commercial leverage. The same engine supports retail rides, reserve rides, standby/hourly service, airport service, errand/grocery service, and membership-based service.
  • It includes operator-grade visibility and in-app education, which lowers onboarding friction and increases salability.

Investor note

This is the value of the software/platform layer by itself. It gets even stronger when paired with live drivers, vehicles, signed enterprise or VIP contracts, or recurring membership revenue. In other words, the software-only valuation is already premium, and the operating company value can sit materially above it.
