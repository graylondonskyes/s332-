What a SkyeCard does

SkyeCards let builders start with useful AI and launch capacity while SkyGate keeps the economics controlled. The card can hold AI credits, pushes, product credit, service credit, and future Skyeverse currency without replacing the existing token/key system.

Credits are internal platform value. They can be used across SkyeSol and SOLEnterprises.org offers, and product credit is intentionally capped and expiring so the system drives action instead of endless unused liability.
